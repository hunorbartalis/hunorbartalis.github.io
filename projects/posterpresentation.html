Project Description

As a part of the ABC Summer School at the University of Amsterdam, our group produced a research proposal and a poster that were presented at the end of the summer school. Our group focused on the following research questions: "What are the long-term effects of photothrombotic stroke in the dorsomedial striatum on the developmental trajectory of visual hemineglect in rats?"
